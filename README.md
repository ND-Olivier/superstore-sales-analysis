# Retail Sales Performance Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data using SQL Server and Power BI to uncover business insights, evaluate sales performance, identify profitable products, understand customer behavior, and support data-driven decision-making.


## 🎯 Business Problem

The business wants to understand:

- Which products generate the highest sales and profit?
- Which categories and regions perform best?
- Which customers contribute the most revenue?
- Which products generate losses?
- How do discounts affect profitability?
- How efficient is the shipping process?

---

## Dataset

- Dataset: Superstore Sales Dataset
- Records: Approximately 10,000
- Includes:
  - Orders
  - Customers

## Power BI Dashboard

The dashboard contains four interactive pages:

### 1️ Executive Dashboard
Provides an overview of sales performance and key business KPIs.

### 2️ Product Performance
Analyzes product sales, profitability, discounts, and top/bottom performing products.

### 3️ Customer & Regional Insights
Explores customer behavior, regions, states, cities, and market distribution.

### 4️ Operational & Shipping Analytics
Analyzes shipping performance, order trends, discounts, quantity, and operational efficiency.

---

## Key Business Insights

- Technology is the highest-performing product category.
- California generates the highest sales.
- New York City leads in total sales among cities.
- Several products generate negative profit.
- Standard Class is the most frequently used shipping mode.
- Large discounts do not always increase profitability.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Increase investment in high-performing products.
- Review products generating negative profit.
- Optimize discount strategies.
- Improve shipping efficiency.
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing regions.
